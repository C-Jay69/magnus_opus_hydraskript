## Design Style
- **Background:** Dark (black/very dark blue)
- **Typography:** Large, bold headlines in light blue gradient
- **Accent Colors:** Purple to cyan gradients
- **Icons:** Simple, rounded, with gradient backgrounds
- **Images:** High-quality, warm, inviting photography
- **Layout:** Split screen - text on left, image on right
- **Buttons:** Gradient fill or outlined
