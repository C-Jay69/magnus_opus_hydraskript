## Design Style
- **Backgrounds:** 
  - Hero: Dark blue/teal gradient with blurred workspace
  - Comparison: Black
  - Plans: Cyan (Starter), Light blue (Author), Dark gray (Publisher)
- **Typography:** 
  - Large, bold headlines in light blue
  - Clean sans-serif
  - Italic emphasis for taglines
- **Icons:** Medal/ribbon icon for each plan tier
- **Accent Colors:** Purple to cyan gradients for buttons
- **Table:** Dark background with green checkmarks, red X's, yellow warning triangles
- **Layout:** Clean, centered, spacious
