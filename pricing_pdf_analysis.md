## Key Design Elements

### Colors by Plan
1. **Starter**: Bright cyan background (#06b6d4), black text
2. **Author**: Black background, light blue header, white text, blue border
3. **Publisher**: Black background, gray header, light blue price, gray border

### Typography
- **Plan titles**: 40-50px, white or black depending on background
- **Prices**: 80-100px, very large and bold
- **Subtitles** (Test the Waters, etc.): Italic, 24-28px
- **Body text**: 18-20px
- **Bullet points**: 16-18px

### Card Structure
- Medal icon at top center
- Plan name + subtitle
- Large price
- Tagline/description
- "Includes:" section with bullet list
- "Best for:" at bottom

### Spacing
- Card padding: 40-50px
- Space between bullets: 12-16px
- Price to description: 30px
- Section gaps: 40px

## Critical Details
1. **Starter has CYAN background** - not black!
2. **Author has blue border** and "Most Popular" badge with star
3. **Publisher has gray theme** - more subdued
4. **Prices are HUGE** - dominant visual element
5. **Medal icons** at top of each card
6. **Comparison table** uses checkmarks, X's, and warning triangles
7. **Hero has blurred workspace background**
8. **"Made with GAMMA" badge** in bottom right (can remove or replace)
